Best Dogs For Families

Dogs are a great addition to any family. But which dog breeds get along best with children? Which breeds are better with young children vs. those that prefer to bond with older kids? Our list of best family dogs can help you choose the best breed for your fam.

Family dogs are just that—dogs who get along with all members of the family, both children and adults. And they’re not just friendly dogs in general. Whether you’re looking for a new family member who’s a gentle giant or one who’s high-energy enough to match the enthusiasm of an active family, we’ve rounded up the 10 best family dogs for those looking to adopt.


10 Best Family Dogs

  1. Beagle
  2. Golden Retriever
  3. Labrador Retriever
  4. Poodle
  5. Irish Setter
  6. Collie
  7. Newfoundland
  8. Bichon Frise
