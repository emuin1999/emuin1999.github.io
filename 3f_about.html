About US

We have created this website to help out dog lovers. There are many dogs lovers all across the globe, some of them with families including children and some without. The purpose of this site is to assist dog lovers with families.

We have worked with different qualified dog trainers with several years of experience. The main philosophy at Furry Family Friend is to provide you with the premier dog training. We give you the tools and skills necessary to train and live harmoniously with your dog, no matter what the problems or the goals are. We believe in a balanced form of training and are constantly learning new skills and techniques so that we can better serve you. Our goal is for you to be able to enjoy living life to its fullest with your furry best friend!
